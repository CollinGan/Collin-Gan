My final module project is called Collin's mood lamp. It is basically a mood lamp with 3 different lights: blue, green and red.

In order to do my final module project, there were various stages. The first stage was to sketch the 3d design using AutoDesk Fusion 360. Here are some pictures of my sketches in Fusion 360.

The second stage was to program the arduino uno. I programmed the arduino uno such that the LED strip will display 3 different lights: green, blue and red. Here is the process:
1.Press the pushbutton
2.Green light flashes.
3. Press the pushbutton.
4.Red light flashes.
5.Press the push button
6.Blue light flashes
7.Press the push button
8.Light turns off

The above video is a demonstration of the process

The third stage is to 3d print a C letter. This C letter represents my name, Collin.

The fourth stage is to laser cut the sketch. I used 3mm transparent and white acryllic.

Now that the sketch has already been laser cut, the fifth stage is to piece the laser cut sides together to form the mood lamp. The C letter will be superglued to the top side of the mood lamp. The LED strip will be placed inside the mood lamp while the arduino uno will be outside of the mood lamp.
